How to delete your account

Users can delete their account directly inside the app after logging in.

  1. Open the app فكر وجاوب صح (Fikr w Gawab Sah).
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Go to your Profile or Settings screen.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

What data is deleted

When a user deletes their account, the following data is deleted from the app account:

  • Account profile information
  • Login credentials associated with the app account
  • User progress and account-related data linked to the account

Data retention

Some limited technical, security, or fraud-prevention records may be retained temporarily only when necessary for security, abuse prevention, or legal compliance purposes. Such records are removed according to our internal retention policies.
